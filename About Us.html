About Us

About Us

Wisdom Public School was Established in the year 2005 in the memory of Capt.Gugan Singh in Badhkhalsa Rai Sonipat with about a lonk experience, the School has worked to inspire youngsters to devote themselves to their betterment and create value in even the most difficult situatuons, which will ensure society's unrestricted and amazing growth.

Wisdom Public School is a symbol of quality education and bolistic all round development. The school employs the latest teaching techniques to ensure that students not only earn certificates, but also accumlate knowledge during their sojourn here.

Wisdom Public School aims to instill a progressive mind-set and muture leader of to,orrow, who will succeed in any endeavour that they turn their hand to, anywhere in the world, and yet retain and unmistakable Indian ethos and Sanskar.

Mission & Vision

Wisdom Public School envisions it's students to be suave, accomplished, academically sound, sporty. adventurous and sophisticated personality before they walk out of it's portals for their next innings of life in the open society. Hence, at Wisdom Public School enhance our standards consistently in order to combine our cilture and hesitage with the best education available.
Wisdom Public School aims to create Clean, Hygenic, academic achievement and pesonal growth. At Wisdom Public School is involved in academic and society relevant programs that uphold fundamental humans values and practice inclusivity and compassion.
Wisdom Public School specially emphasis on growing students personlity through a variety of co-curricular and on-stage activities throughout their tenure at the school.
